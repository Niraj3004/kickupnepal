The Platform Nepal's
Sneakerheads Deserve

We noticed that Nepal's sneaker community had to rely on expensive grey-market sources, with no guarantee of authenticity. KickUpNepal was founded in 2022 to change that.

We source directly from authorised distributors across Asia, verify every single pair before it ships, and deliver across all 77 districts of Nepal — from Kathmandu to Pokhara to EBC Base Camp.
