Place the sneakers back in the original box with all accessories, tags, and dust bags. Unworn condition only.

Policy Details

All items that are unworn, in original packaging with all tags attached. Sale items are also eligible within the 7-day window.

Yes! We allow exchanges for different products. If there's a price difference, we'll charge or refund accordingly.
