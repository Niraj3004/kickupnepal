Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?expand_more
Go to Track Order and enter your order ID. You'll receive live SMS updates from our courier partners.
Can I cancel my order?expand_more
Yes, orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After dispatch, you'll need to initiate a return. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp.
What's your return policy?expand_more
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, in original box with all tags intact. See our Returns Policy.
How long does delivery take?expand_more
Kathmandu Valley: 1–2 business days. Other cities: 2–5 days. Remote areas: up to 10 days. Free shipping on all orders.
Do you sell authentic products?expand_more
100% yes. Every single product is authenticated by our trained team before dispatch. We issue authenticity certificates on request.
Which payment methods do you accept?expand_more
eSewa, Khalti, bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and Cash on Delivery (COD) available across Nepal.
Can I exchange for a different size?expand_more
Yes! Size exchanges are free of charge within 7 days. Contact us on WhatsApp with your order number and desired size.
Do you ship outside Nepal?expand_more
Currently we only ship within Nepal. International shipping is coming soon!
